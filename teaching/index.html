Lectures (L), Directed Work (DW), Practical Work (PW).

Total: > 240 Hours in Cognitive psychology, Ergonomics, Tools and Methodology of science, Cognitive Neuroscience, Neurophysiology in French and English

 

Ghent University (Belgium) - Total: 100h

  • Master in Theoretical and Experimental Psychology: Modelling of Cognitive Processes (M1, 27h L and 72h PW; over 3 years from 2018 to 2021) - Presentation of the computational modelling approach in psychology, and implementation of neural network models in Python.
  • Master in Biochemistry and Biotechnology: Neuroimaging techniques (M2, 1h L). Presentation of invasive and non-invasive neuroimaging techniques for use in humans and non-human animals.

 

Master of Neurosciences - UFR of Fundamental and Biomedical Sciences - University of Paris (France) - Total: 10h

  • Neurophysiology of the visual system (M1; 4h L, from 2019 to 2021) - Presentation of the visual system and the processing stages from the retina to the ventral and dorsal pathways.
  • Attentional networks (M1; 4h L, from 2019 to 2021) - Bases of the behavioral study of attention.
  • Methodology of article analysis (M1; 2h DW, from 2019 to 2021) - Methodology of analysis of scientific articles in experimental psychology and cognitive neuroscience.

 

Bachelor of Biology (Biomedical Sciences) - UFR of Fundamental and Biomedical Sciences - University of Paris (France) - Total: 12h

  • Neuroimaging: Evoked potentials in Electroencephalography (EEG) (L2; 6h DW, 2020) - Reminder of the lecture on EEG, description of the principle of evoked potentials and practice on a set of sensory stimulation data.
  • Neuroimaging: Retinotopy in functional Magnetic Resonance Imaging (fMRI) (L2; 6h DW, 2020) - Reminder of the lecture on fMRI, practice on a retinotopic protocol scenario and delineation of primary visual areas on maps of BOLD activations.

 

Master in Aeronautical Engineering - ISAE-Supaéro (France) - Total 49h

  • Attention and executive functions (M1; 9h L, from 2018 to 2021) - Types of attention involved for an aeronautical operator and executive functions allowing attentional control. Cases in which these processes fail (e.g. tunneling)
  • Memory and learning: behavioral and neurobiological bases (M1; 15h L, from 2017 to 2021) - Mechanisms and types of memories and learning and brain structures involved.
  • Methodology of article analysis (M1; 4h DW, from 2020 to 2021) - Methodology of analysis of scientific articles in experimental psychology, ergonomics and cognitive neuroscience.
  • Introduction to the experimental method in ergonomics (M1; 3h DW, 2017) - Psychometric and statistical methods used for the design of an experiment and the analysis of behavioral data.
  • Introduction to programming in Python (M1; 10h L and 10 PW, 2017) - Presentation of the structure of the Python programming language and practical exercises.

 

Bachelor in Biology - Toulouse 3 Paul Sabatier University (France) - Total: 46h

  • Limbic system: physiological regulation of emotions (L2; 10h DW, 2013) - Description of the anatomy and functions of the limbic system and the hypothalamus.
  • Perceptual pain thresholds in humans (L2; 28h PW, from 2012 to 2014) - Measurement of perceptual pain thresholds in humans and use of a placebo manipulation.
  • Anxiety in mice (L2; 8h Lab, 2014) - Measure of mice behaviors reflecting anxiety in different environments (open field or raised cross maze).

 

Toulouse Orthoptic School (France) - Total: 24h

  • Psychophysiology: sensation, perception, memory and learning (L1; 12h L, from 2012 to 2014) - Distinction between perception and sensation. Mechanisms and types of memories and learning and brain structures involved.
  • Sensory receptors (L1; 12h L, from 2012 to 2014) - Neurophysiological structures and mechanisms of sensory receptors (e.g. cochlea, olfactory bulb).
